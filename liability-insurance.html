Liability Insurance

A customer slips on your premises. A product causes harm. A client alleges your advice led to financial loss. A data breach exposes sensitive information. In each of these situations, your business faces legal claims — and the costs of defending them, settling them, or losing in court can be severe. Liability Insurance makes sure a single incident doesn't become an existential threat to your business.

What's Covered

  • Public Liability: covers claims for bodily injury or property damage caused to third parties through your business operations or premises.
  • Product Liability: protects manufacturers, distributors, and retailers against claims arising from defective or harmful products.
  • Employer's Liability: covers your legal obligations if an employee is injured or dies at work.
  • Professional Indemnity (E&O): essential for consultants, lawyers, doctors, IT firms, and financial advisors — covers negligence, omission, and incorrect advice claims.
  • Commercial General Liability (CGL): a comprehensive package covering bodily injury, property damage, personal injury, and advertising liability.
  • Cyber Liability: covers financial losses from data breaches, ransomware attacks, regulatory penalties, and IT-related third-party claims.
  • Directors & Officers (D&O): protects senior executives from personal financial loss arising from decisions made in their management capacity.
  • Legal defense costs included: court fees, lawyer costs, settlements, and judgments are all within scope — not just the final payout.

Why It Matters

One claim won't sink your business: even minor legal disputes generate significant defense costs — liability cover absorbs that exposure.
Keep operations running through disputes: legal battles take time; your business doesn't have to pause while they play out.
Meet contractual and regulatory requirements: clients, tenders, and regulators increasingly require proof of liability coverage as a condition of doing business.
Protect personal assets of directors: D&O cover ensures that personal wealth isn't at stake when business decisions are challenged.
Tailored to your industry: coverage is structured around your specific risk profile — not a generic policy applied across sectors.

Who Should Consider This

IT and consulting firms, healthcare providers, manufacturers, real estate developers, retailers, financial services businesses, startups with board-level governance, and any organization that interacts with clients, handles data, or has employees working on-site.
