Health Insurance

A single medical emergency can wipe out years of savings. Health Insurance ensures that when you or your family members fall ill or meet with an accident, the focus remains on recovery, not the hospital bill. It provides you with access to quality healthcare at private hospitals across India, with the insurer picking up the tab for everything from room rent to complex surgeries.

What's Covered

  • Hospitalization coverage: covers room rent, ICU charges, surgeon fees, diagnostics, and medicines for any inpatient treatment lasting over 24 hours.
  • Pre & Post-hospitalization: expenses incurred up to 60 days before admission and 90 days after discharge are reimbursed.
  • Daycare procedures: coverage for modern medical treatments that require less than 24 hours of hospitalization, like cataracts or dialysis.
  • Emergency ambulance: costs for transporting the patient to the hospital in an emergency are covered.
  • No Claim Bonus (NCB): your sum insured increases for every year you don't make a claim, at no extra cost.
  • Annual health check-ups: many plans offer complimentary preventive health check-ups once a year.
  • Tax benefits: premiums paid for self, family, and parents qualify for tax deductions under Section 80D.

Why It Matters

Access the best hospitals without worry: choose the healthcare provider you trust, not just the one you can afford at that moment.
Protect your life's savings: medical inflation in India is rising fast — a comprehensive policy keeps your wealth intact.
Cashless treatment: with a network of thousands of hospitals, you don't need to arrange large amounts of cash during an emergency.
Significant tax savings: reduce your tax liability while securing your family's health — a double win under Section 80D.

Who Should Consider This

Every individual and family — from young professionals starting out to senior citizens. If you have dependents, a family floater plan is essential; if you're an employee with corporate cover, a personal 'top-up' plan adds a critical extra layer of safety.
