Group Travel Insurance

When your team travels — for client meetings, conferences, project deployments, or trade visits — they take on a set of risks that don't exist in the office: medical emergencies in unfamiliar cities, cancelled flights, lost documents, delayed baggage. Group Travel Insurance covers all of it under one policy, so your team can focus on the purpose of the trip rather than managing what could go wrong.

What's Covered

  • Emergency medical coverage: hospitalization, treatment, medical evacuation, and repatriation of remains — covered wherever your team travels.
  • Trip cancellation and interruption: non-refundable bookings are reimbursed when travel is disrupted by illness, accidents, or unforeseen events.
  • Baggage loss and delay: compensation for lost, stolen, or significantly delayed luggage and personal belongings.
  • Passport loss: costs for obtaining emergency replacement travel documentation are covered.
  • Personal liability: protection if a traveler accidentally injures a third party or damages property during the trip.
  • Accidental death and disability: lump sum benefit if a covered traveler is fatally injured or permanently disabled during travel.
  • 24/7 global assistance: a round-the-clock helpline for medical referrals, hospital coordination, and emergency support — wherever in the world your team is.
  • Coverage zones: domestic India, Asia-only, or worldwide (including or excluding USA/Canada) — selected to match your travel patterns.

Why It Matters

Your team is never on their own: 24/7 global support means a medical emergency or travel disruption abroad doesn't become a solo crisis.
Protects your travel investment: trip cancellation coverage recovers non-refundable costs that would otherwise be written off.
More affordable than individual per-trip policies: covering a group under one policy is significantly cheaper than everyone arranging their own cover.
Fulfils your duty of care: as an employer sending staff into unfamiliar environments, providing travel cover is both a legal and ethical responsibility.
Customizable to your travel profile: configure by destination, trip duration, traveler age, and risk level — so you pay for exactly what you need.

Who Should Consider This

Businesses that send teams on domestic or international trips — corporate sales and client-facing roles, export-import companies, educational institutions running exchange programs, NGOs with field operations, and tour operators managing group client travel.
