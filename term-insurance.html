Term Insurance

Term Insurance is the simplest and most powerful financial tool you can put in place for the people who depend on you. If you pass away during the policy term, your family receives a large lump sum — enough to replace your income, pay off outstanding loans, fund your children's education, and maintain the life you built together. It delivers the highest level of financial protection at the lowest possible premium of any life insurance product.

What's Covered

  • High sum assured at low cost: a ₹1 crore cover for a healthy 30-year-old can cost under ₹10,000 per year.
  • Flexible premium payment: pay monthly, quarterly, semi-annually, or annually — structured to fit your cash flow.
  • Long policy tenure: coverage terms of up to 40 years — lock in a low premium while you're young and keep it for decades.
  • Critical illness rider: receive a lump sum on diagnosis of conditions like cancer, heart attack, or stroke — without waiting for death.
  • Accidental death benefit: an additional payout if the cause of death is an accident — on top of the base sum assured.
  • Waiver of premium rider: if you suffer a critical illness or permanent disability, future premiums are waived and the policy stays active.
  • Tax benefit: premiums qualify for deduction under Section 80C; the death benefit is tax-free under Section 10(10D).

Why It Matters

Your liabilities don't become your family's burden: home loans, business debts, and personal guarantees are covered by the payout — not passed on.
The most affordable form of life protection: no savings or investment component inflating the premium — pure, efficient risk cover.
Immediate peace of mind: once the policy is active, your family is protected — regardless of what happens to you.
Tax savings on top of protection: every rupee of premium reduces your taxable income under Section 80C.

Who Should Consider This

Working professionals with financial dependents, individuals with home loans or business liabilities, parents planning for their children's future, and anyone who wants to ensure their family's financial stability regardless of what happens to them.
