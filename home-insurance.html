Home Insurance

Your home is likely your most valuable financial asset and the center of your family's life. But it's also vulnerable to risks you can't control: fire, earthquakes, floods, and burglary. Home Insurance ensures that if your property is damaged or your belongings are stolen, you have the immediate financial means to repair, rebuild, and replace — without starting from zero.

What's Covered

  • Structure coverage: covers the cost of repairing or rebuilding the physical walls, roof, and foundation following damage by fire or natural perils.
  • Contents and belongings: protection for your furniture, electronics, appliances, and clothing against theft, fire, and accidental damage.
  • Jewelry and valuables: optional extra cover for precious items, even when they're kept in a home locker or worn outside.
  • Natural calamities: protection against earthquake, flood, storm, and lightning — essential for homes in high-risk zones.
  • Public liability: covers your legal obligations if a visitor is injured on your property or if your domestic help meets with an accident at work.
  • Alternate accommodation: if your home becomes uninhabitable due to a covered event, the policy pays for your temporary rent.
  • Terrorism cover: protection for your property against damage caused by acts of terrorism.

Why It Matters

Total protection for owners and tenants: whether you own the building or just the belongings inside, there is a policy structured for your needs.
Highest coverage for the lowest premium: insuring a home worth crores typically costs less than insuring a mid-range car.
Fast recovery from disaster: rebuild and refurnish your home using insurance funds rather than depleting your lifetime's savings.
Comprehensive safety net: from a broken TV to a total structural loss, your policy acts as a broad shield for your family's lifestyle.

Who Should Consider This

Homeowners, apartment owners in high-rise societies, and tenants who want to protect their furniture and expensive electronics. If you have an active home loan, structure insurance is usually a mandatory bank requirement.
