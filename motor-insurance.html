Motor Insurance

In India, Motor Insurance is a legal mandate — but for the smart vehicle owner, it's a vital financial shield. Whether it's a minor fender-bender or a major accident, the cost of repairs and third-party liabilities can be overwhelming. Motor Insurance ensures that you can stay on the road without the fear of sudden, high out-of-pocket expenses.

What's Covered

  • Own Damage (OD) cover: pays for repairs to your vehicle resulting from accidents, fire, theft, or natural disasters.
  • Third-Party Liability: covers your legal and financial obligations if your vehicle causes injury, death, or property damage to someone else.
  • Zero Depreciation add-on: ensures you receive the full cost of replacement parts without any deduction for aging — essential for new cars.
  • Engine Protection: covers repair costs for engine damage due to water ingression (hydrostatic lock) or oil leakage.
  • Roadside Assistance (RSA): 24/7 help for towing, flat tires, battery jump-starts, and emergency fuel — wherever you're stranded.
  • Return to Invoice (RTI): in case of total loss or theft, the insurer pays the full original invoice price, not just the depreciated market value.
  • Key replacement: reimburses the cost of replacing lost or stolen vehicle keys and locks.

Why It Matters

Legal compliance made easy: drive with the confidence that you're meeting all statutory requirements under the Motor Vehicles Act.
Cashless repairs at top garages: access a wide network of authorized service centers where the insurer settles the bill directly.
Reward for safe driving: earn significant No Claim Bonus (NCB) discounts on your premium for every year you drive without a claim.
Stress-free claims: our experts assist you through the survey and documentation process to ensure your vehicle is back on the road fast.

Who Should Consider This

Every vehicle owner in India. Whether you have a new luxury car, a family SUV, or a two-wheeler, having the right mix of OD and TP coverage is the only way to protect your mobility and your wallet.
